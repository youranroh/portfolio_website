Unit Converter

This is a personal project I have made to practice JAVA's GUI and JFrames.

First, the User must select the unit they want to convert.

Then they must select the unit they want to convert from and the unit they want to convert to.

Press "convert" then the converter will do its magic and convert the numbers!
